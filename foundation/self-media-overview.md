# Self-Media: Definition, Classification, and Platforms

## 1. Definition of Self-Media

**Self-media** refers to digital content channels that are independently created, managed, and distributed by individuals or small teams, rather than traditional media organizations.

It is characterized by:
- Decentralized content creation
- Direct audience engagement
- Platform-based distribution (e.g., apps and websites)
- Low barrier to entry

In practice, self-media creators produce content such as videos, articles, podcasts, or live streams and publish them on social media platforms.

---

## 2. Classification of Self-Media

Self-media can be categorized based on content type and purpose:

### 2.1 By Content Format

- **Short-form Video**
    - Fast-paced, highly engaging content (e.g., TikTok-style videos)

- **Long-form Video**
    - More in-depth content such as vlogs, documentaries, or tutorials

- **Text-Based Content**
    - Articles, blogs, essays, and written posts

- **Image-Based Content**
    - Photo sharing, visual storytelling

- **Live Streaming**
    - Real-time interaction with audiences

- **Audio Content**
    - Podcasts, voice-based platforms

---

### 2.2 By Content Purpose

- **Entertainment Content**
    - Comedy, lifestyle, storytelling

- **Informational Content**
    - Tutorials, education, industry insights

- **Opinion-Based Content**
    - Commentary, personal viewpoints, analysis

- **Social/Interactive Content**
    - Street interviews, Q&A, public reactions

- **Personal Branding Content**
    - Focused on building a personal image or identity

---

### 2.3 By Creator Type

- **Individual Creators**
    - Independent content producers

- **Professional Creators**
    - Full-time influencers or content teams

- **Business/Brand Accounts**
    - Companies using self-media for marketing

---

## 3. Most Common Self-Media Platforms in China

Below are the major self-media platforms widely used in China:

### 3.1 Short Video Platforms

- **Douyin (Chinese TikTok)**
    - The most dominant short-video platform
    - Strong algorithm-driven content distribution

- **Kuaishou**
    - Focuses more on grassroots and real-life content
    - Strong user loyalty

---

### 3.2 Lifestyle & Community Platforms

- **Xiaohongshu (RED)**
    - Focus on lifestyle, fashion, beauty, and product sharing
    - Strong influence on purchasing decisions

---

### 3.3 Video Platforms

- **Bilibili**
    - Popular among younger audiences
    - Known for longer videos, education, and niche communities

---

### 3.4 Social Media Platforms

- **Weibo**
    - Open social platform similar to Twitter
    - Good for trending topics and public discussions

- **WeChat Official Accounts**
    - Article-based content distribution
    - Strong for private traffic and long-term audience retention

---

## 4. Key Takeaways

- Self-media empowers individuals to become independent content creators.
- It combines content production, distribution, and audience interaction.
- Content can be classified by format, purpose, and creator type.
- In China, platforms like Douyin, Xiaohongshu, and Bilibili dominate the self-media ecosystem.
- Short-form video is currently the most competitive and fastest-growing format.
