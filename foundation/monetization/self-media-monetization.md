# Self-Media Monetization Guide

## 1. What is Self-Media Monetization?

Self-media monetization refers to the process where individuals or small teams create content (videos, articles, posts) and generate income through audience attention, influence, and traffic.

Core logic:
> Content → Traffic → Trust → Monetization

---

## 2. Main Ways to Earn Money

### 2.1 Platform Revenue (Traffic-Based)

- Ads revenue (e.g. YouTube AdSense, Bilibili incentives)
- Creator funds / traffic subsidies (Douyin, Kuaishou)

✅ Pros:
- Passive income
- No need to sell anything

❌ Cons:
- Requires large traffic
- Low revenue per view

👉 Typical:
- 10k–100k views ≈ small income
- Needs scale

---

### 2.2 Advertising / Brand Collaboration

- Sponsored content
- Product placement
- Brand campaigns

✅ Pros:
- High income per deal
- Scales with influence

❌ Cons:
- Requires followers & credibility

👉 Typical:
- 10k–50k followers → small ads
- 100k+ → stable brand deals

---

### 2.3 E-commerce (Very Important)

- Sell products (own or affiliate)
- Livestream selling
- Product recommendations

✅ Pros:
- Highest earning potential
- Direct monetization

❌ Cons:
- Requires conversion ability
- Needs trust

👉 This is usually the **BIGGEST income source**

---

### 2.4 Knowledge / Service Monetization

- Paid courses
- Consulting
- Memberships
- Paid communities

✅ Pros:
- High margin
- Stable income

❌ Cons:
- Requires expertise

---

### 2.5 Traffic Redirection (Private Domain)

- Redirect to WeChat / private channels
- Sell services or products

👉 Very common in China ecosystem

---

## 3. Which One Earns the Most?

Ranking (generally):

1. **E-commerce / Selling products**
2. **Advertising / Brand deals**
3. **Knowledge / services**
4. **Platform traffic revenue**

👉 Key insight:
> Traffic itself does NOT make the most money — conversion does.

---

## 4. Is 200,000 RMB/year Possible?

👉 YES — very realistic

Breakdown:
- 200,000 RMB / year
- ≈ 16,600 RMB / month
- ≈ 550 RMB / day

This is achievable with:
- Small but stable audience
- Basic monetization strategy

## 5. Practical Path to 200K/year

### Phase 1: Positioning (0–1 month)

Choose a niche:
- Real-life content (your idea)
- Street interviews
- Relationships / daily life
- Humor + realism

👉 Key:
- Clear audience
- Repeatable format

---

### Phase 2: Content Growth (1–3 months)

Goal:
- 1–2 videos per day OR consistent posting
- Test formats

Focus:
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Real / relatable topics
- Short + engaging

Target:
- 1–10k followers

---

### Phase 3: First Monetization (3–6 months)

Start with:

#### Option A: Ads
- Small business promotions

#### Option B: Affiliate products
- Recommend useful items

#### Option C: Traffic redirect
- Build private audience

Target:
- First $500–$1000/month

---

### Phase 4: Scale Income (6–12 months)

Focus:

- Build trust
- Improve conversion
- Optimize content

Main income:
- Product selling
- Brand deals

---

## 7. Key Success Factors

### 7.1 Content
- Real
- Relatable
- Emotional or funny

### 7.2 Consistency
- Posting frequency matters more than perfection

### 7.3 Conversion Ability
- Not just views → ability to sell

---

## 8. Common Mistakes

- Only chasing views (no monetization plan)
- No niche / unclear positioning
- Posting inconsistently
- Ignoring audience trust

---

## 9. Final Insight

> Small audience + strong trust > large audience + weak conversion

---

## 10. Simple Plan Summary

1. Pick a niche (real-life content)
2. Post consistently (daily if possible)
3. Reach 10k followers
4. Start small monetization
5. Scale to products or ads

---

## Conclusion

- Self-media earning is very achievable
- 200K/year is realistic
